Insights & Recommendations

Opportunities for AI Call Deflection

Based on the call volume and the nature of inquiries, several categories present strong opportunities for deflection using a Conversational AI agent. Automating these routine queries can free up staff to handle more complex issues, improving overall efficiency.

  • Property Enquiry (Availability/Pricing): With 161 calls, this is the largest sub-category. An AI can be trained to check property availability and provide pricing information from a database or CRM, answering these common questions instantly.
  • Property Enquiry (Viewing/Inspection): The 41 calls in this category could be handled by an AI that schedules inspections based on pre-defined availability, integrating directly with a calendar system.
  • General Enquiry: Many general questions about opening hours, addresses, and services can be easily answered by a well-trained AI, reducing the need for human intervention for these simple queries.

Implementation Steps

  1. Knowledge Base Integration: Connect the AI to a knowledge base containing property details, pricing, and general company information.
  2. Calendar Integration: Integrate with a scheduling system to allow the AI to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
  3. CRM Integration: For more complex queries, the AI can create a ticket or log the interaction in a CRM for a human agent to follow up.
